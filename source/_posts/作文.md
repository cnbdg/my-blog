## 📖 英语作文原文
<p align="center">My Hobby - Calligraphy</p>
I enjoy several free-time activities, such as painting, writing poems, and cooking, but nothing beats doing calligraphy.

I started it when I was ten years old. My mom taught me how to write it at first. It was difficult for me, but now I spend many hours practicing it a lot, and I can write it beautifully.

Doing calligraphy is good for me in many ways. First of all, it can help me calm down and reduce my stress after a busy day. What’s more, it gives me a strong sense of achievement when I write well. Most importantly, it makes me more patient in my studies.

As the saying goes, "Activities make life colorful."

Doing calligraphy is an important part of my life.
